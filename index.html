N-Gram Predictor

About the Project

This is a n-Gram based word predictor which uses upto 4 grams.
Corpora Used (Jane Austen Novels):

  • Pride and Prejudice
  • Sense and Sensibility
The corpus is pre-processed by removing all the white spaces and other symbols and n-Grams are generated. The sentence that is given as input is split into tokens and our prediction algorithm predicts the next word using Good-Turing frequency estimation and two smoothing algorithms Backoff and Interpolation.
